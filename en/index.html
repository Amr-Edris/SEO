About Us

DentLink is a community-based platform that connects dental students with real patients who need dental treatments, helping them complete clinical requirements in a structured and supervised environment.

We focus on supporting dental students and providing everything they need to complete their work efficiently.

Our goal is to reduce the difficulty of finding suitable cases and provide a professional experience that benefits both students and patients.
